Stages of Hair Fall (Grade 1 - 7)

Identify your stage and track progress weekly with consistent photos.

MildModerateSevere
Stage 1

Mild shedding

No visible thinning. Hairline looks normal.

Stage 2

Early recession

Slight thinning / mild temple recession.

Stage 3

Noticeable thinning

Clear hairline recession or crown thinning.

Stage 4

Crown + front thinning

More pronounced recession + thinning at crown.

Stage 5

Extensive thinning

Front + crown thinning increases; bridge becomes thin.

Stage 6

Large bald area

Front and crown areas nearly connect.

Stage 7

Severe pattern baldness

Mostly bald scalp; hair remains on sides/back.

Tip: For best tracking, take photos in same lighting + same angle every week.
